# Subscription, billing, and upgrade

The **Subscription** page is the entry point for managing your i-doit up plan and the billing details that go with it.

It lives under **Settings ▸ Administration ▸ Subscription** in the Settings sidebar, or directly via the purple **Upgrade** button at the top of every page.

## Sections

The page groups three concerns: the active plan, the billing address, and add-on access.

### Plan

Shows the current tier (for example *Free*, *Standard*, *Plus*) and the limits associated with it, number of objects, users, tenants, and any feature gates.
A **Change plan** action moves you to a higher or lower tier; a confirmation dialog explains the price difference and the next billing date before the change takes effect.

### Billing

The **Billing** card surfaces:

- the **payment method** on file (credit card, SEPA mandate, invoice, depending on the region);
- the **billing address** used on every invoice;
- the **invoice history** with a download link per invoice.

Editing billing details opens a side panel; saving updates the next invoice automatically.

### Add-ons

Each available add-on is listed with its activation state (*Active*, *Trial active until …*, *Available with upgrade*).
Buttons offer **Activate**, **Free Trial**, or **Upgrade** depending on the add-on's licensing status.

The same list is also reachable directly from the user menu, see [Add-ons](addons.md).

## When the page is gated

Your role decides whether you can change anything on this page.
Read access is broad; making purchases, changing plan, or editing the billing address requires the *Manage Subscription* right under *Administration*: see [Rights and permissions](rights-and-permissions.md).

## The Upgrade button

The **Upgrade** button in the top bar always points here, even for users without write rights.
For users without rights it shows the plan and the upgrade options as a read-only call-to-action; for billing administrators it doubles as the shortcut to the management form.
