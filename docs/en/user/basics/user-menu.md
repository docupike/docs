# User menu

The **user menu** is the dropdown that opens when you click your avatar (the coloured circle with your initial) at the far top-right of every page.

## Entries

The menu has four entries.
A thin horizontal divider sits between **Add-ons** and **Logout** to set *Logout* apart as the destructive action.

| Entry | What it does |
|---|---|
| **Give feedback** | Opens a feedback form so you can send improvement requests, bug reports, or general comments to the i-doit up team. |
| **Settings** | Navigates to `/settings/`. The Settings page shows the same sidebar covered by the [Profile](profile.md), [User management](../../admin/user-management.md), [Tenants](../../admin/tenants.md), [CMDB Configuration](../../admin/class-collection-management.md), and other admin pages. |
| **Add-ons** | Lists every add-on available to the tenant and lets you toggle, configure, or trial them, see [Add-ons](../../admin/addons.md). |
| **Logout** | Ends the current session immediately. |

## Tips

- The menu closes when you click outside the panel or press **Escape**.
- The avatar's letter is the first letter of your first name (or username).
  The colour is generated from your account and is stable across sessions.

## Related entry points

- The avatar is the only place the **Logout** action lives, there is no separate sign-out button elsewhere.
- The **Settings** entry is the most common navigation destination from this menu; the path is identical to typing `/settings/` in the URL bar.
